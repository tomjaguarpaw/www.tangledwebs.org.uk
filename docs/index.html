TangledWebs UK

TangledWebs UK is a group supporting the rights of donor-conceived people in the UK and across the world.

The TangledWebs UK website has two purposes:

Firstly, to present the negative side of donor-conception from the point of view of those who are touched by it, especially donor-conceived people themselves.

Secondly, to be a resource centre for donor-conceived people who have recently discovered this fact, or have only recently become aware of its negative consequences. You can find links to various means of support.
